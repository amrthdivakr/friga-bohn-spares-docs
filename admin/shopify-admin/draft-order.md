# How to Access a Specific Shopify Draft Order
Learn how to quickly locate and open a saved draft order within your Shopify admin dashboard. This guide simplifies navigation so you can efficiently manage your pending customer transactions.

1\. Navigate to [Shopify Admin](https://admin.shopify.com/store/friga-bohn-spares-store)

![](../images/shopify-admin/draft-order/step-01.jpeg)


2\. Click **Orders**

![](../images/shopify-admin/draft-order/step-02.jpeg)


3\. Click **Drafts**

![](../images/shopify-admin/draft-order/step-03.jpeg)


4\. View any Drafts by clicking one

![](../images/shopify-admin/draft-order/step-04.jpeg)

[Go back to Shopify Admin](../shopify-admin.md)
