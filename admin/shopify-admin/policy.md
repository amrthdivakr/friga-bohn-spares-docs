# Adding a Policy pages to the website
#### [Made by Amruth Divakar with Scribe](https://scribehow.com/o/AmjRagUGQxOh31NKNgqRAQ/viewer/Adding_a_Policy_pages_to_the_website__hoc1cIxpRWWCkfyhFfBrfg)
Learn how to easily link your store's privacy policy to your website's footer menu using the Shopify admin dashboard. This guide simplifies the process of configuring your footer navigation to ensure your legal pages are easily accessible to customers.

1\. Navigate to [https://admin.shopify.com/store/friga-bohn-spares-store](https://admin.shopify.com/store/friga-bohn-spares-store)

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/876908e6-a3c3-40e0-b223-f84de6b380f1/ascreenshot_b2dd95c4a28c48948c6adcad032da980_text_export.jpeg)


2\. Click "Settings"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/876908e6-a3c3-40e0-b223-f84de6b380f1/ascreenshot_ca29ce0f4c93433a8999a4ca4960b3a9_text_export.jpeg)


3\. Click "Policies"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/76096364-c648-4680-a4be-4c57c778a3ba/ascreenshot_206a8e4d7af04c8bb3ce3d6c3d1437e7_text_export.jpeg)


4\. Add/Edit any available Policies

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/9bdfe098-c0af-461e-8bf2-5fe9da85552f/ascreenshot_229589db2a0f42c39df53d103345eb83_text_export.jpeg)


5\. Click this button to close Settings

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/2b61b6ab-91b8-4d4c-a316-96f663750622/ascreenshot_659390f234c9404db58bb4834de9a724_text_export.jpeg)


6\. Click "Content"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/4e1fb27a-aafc-476e-a311-91f6f780d27d/ascreenshot_4e88279670a448428a8a7c045be0ed05_text_export.jpeg)


7\. Click "Menus"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/8d69d360-51ee-4a37-8e66-fc7e9d49c528/ascreenshot_74c2c6865cce412bbee6b1aa877ac16c_text_export.jpeg)


8\. Click "Footer menu"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/97810a2b-bd92-471a-a97d-cfd3248a70f5/ascreenshot_42de7286cb5a41cabb25ce328e1939be_text_export.jpeg)


9\. Click "Add menu item"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/1e9ffc8c-ce81-42c0-9f4d-32693fba506e/ascreenshot_0069d0ed855f44b7a2e0e7d88ea08651_text_export.jpeg)


10\. Click the "Link" field.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/76c050ed-0a30-4a2c-9961-9880ed44fa3a/ascreenshot_9f303d2958b04181a6a20c42705d0711_text_export.jpeg)


11\. Click "Policies" and add policies to the menu which will appear in the website Footer.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/4d462d26-5cad-49f7-b61a-e0b86a9446d9/ascreenshot_675e94f7709b417888c72fc0c7c600ab_text_export.jpeg)


12\. To add Translations to the Policies, Click "Apps"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/95b06aa0-2a55-4f27-942c-815a08f0b5aa/ascreenshot_106dc4152f094df19b216408a9f0e183_text_export.jpeg)


13\. Click "Translate & Adapt"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/14f738fa-11e4-477e-a3f6-f8853ace6c1a/ascreenshot_794f230b0f3844efb63201e508a8639f_text_export.jpeg)


14\. Click "Policies"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/2c661c80-edac-4281-9242-c0581cf9475b/ascreenshot_82aa3c8f85244280b2fd8062038b25b6_text_export.jpeg)
