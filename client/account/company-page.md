# How to Add a Company Location and set Price Visibility 
#### [Made by Amruth Divakar with Scribe](https://scribehow.com/o/AmjRagUGQxOh31NKNgqRAQ/viewer/How_to_Add_a_Company_Location_and_set_Price_Visibility__w73XW5giQCiJVgUctWJkvQ)
Learn how to manage your business operations by adding new locations to your company profile. This guide provides a simple walkthrough for inputting address details and configuring catalog price restrictions for your company users.(Company page access only available for Distributor Admins)

1\. Navigate to [[Accounts]] Page

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/af53cedc-4ae6-4c5f-82f8-a954cfcc56ed/ascreenshot_a7bda8677eff4880bead09dc6ddaa3b9_text_export.jpeg)


2\. Click "Company"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/af53cedc-4ae6-4c5f-82f8-a954cfcc56ed/ascreenshot_1329ac2be0224553b084e0ecea8264c9_text_export.jpeg)


3\. Click "Add location"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/6046a85d-5e28-47e0-8b1b-7439ac99c84c/ascreenshot_2426010aed5342b9876dffca9a68da02_text_export.jpeg)


4\. Fill the form and click [[Create location]] to add a new location

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/4973515e-9d4a-4a7a-a01e-8b2207f767d5/ascreenshot_e19f575d56a348889ed6709841917428_text_export.jpeg)


5\. Set Price Visibility to [[On]], to hide the catalog prices from Distributor and Distributor Location Admin roles

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-22/2de18655-3108-499b-93f1-7d1ffecb0889/ascreenshot_f16f00c042b448138f9666e3a2f84af3_text_export.jpeg)
