## Home

### Search by model

[Step by step visual guide](https://scribehow.com/o/AmjRagUGQxOh31NKNgqRAQ/viewer/How_to_Search_for_Models_on_the_Website__6XiZ8xSFQ8-AuznDnM7Row)

- Choose **Models** in the dropdown
- Type model name or number
- Click Search (or press enter)
- Opens Models browse with your search

### Search by part

[Step by step visual guide](https://scribehow.com/o/AmjRagUGQxOh31NKNgqRAQ/viewer/How_to_Search_for_Parts_on_Your_Shopify_Store__hoRfxsbbSeK3HRK45XqvsQ)

- Choose **Parts** in the dropdown
- Type part name or number
- Click Search
- Opens Parts catalogue with your search

### Browse without searching

- Click **Models** → Models browse page
- Click **Parts** → Parts browse page

## Models browse (`/browse`)

[Step by step visual guide](https://scribehow.com/o/AmjRagUGQxOh31NKNgqRAQ/viewer/How_To_Search_For_Models_On_Your_Storefront__nle4v2HSTBq6tAqXBg09sw)

## Part page (`/parts/...`)

[Step by step visual guide](https://scribehow.com/o/AmjRagUGQxOh31NKNgqRAQ/viewer/Managing_Parts_and_Cart_Selections_on_Part_page__uxuX3GaYSS6_wyRQlXJIYQ)

### Go back to model parts list

- Click the back link (model name) to view other parts for this model

### Change quantity

- Use the quantity dropdown

### Add to cart

- Set quantity
- Click **Add to cart**

### Remove from cart (already added)

- Click **Remove from cart**

### Update quantity (already in cart)

- Change quantity in the dropdown — cart updates automatically

### Open compatibility PDF

- Click the model tag under compatible models (when PDF exists)

### Open another part for the same model

- Scroll to **Other parts for …**
- Click a card
