# Catalogue

## Home

### Search by model

- Choose **Models** in the dropdown
- Type model name or number
- Click Search (or press enter)
- Opens Models browse with your search

### Search by part

- Choose **Parts** in the dropdown
- Type part name or number
- Click Search
- Opens Parts catalogue with your search

### Browse without searching

- Click **Browse** → Models browse page

---

## Models browse (`/browse`)

### Search models

- Type in the search field
- Submit → results update

### Filter by category

- Desktop: pick a category in the sidebar
- Mobile: tap **Filters**, pick a category, close drawer
- Tap again to clear filter

### Sort the list

- New / A–Z / Z–A

### Open a model

- Click a model card

### Change page

- Use pagination at the bottom (previous / next)

---

## Model page (`/models/...`)

### Go back to all models

- Click **Browse** (back link)

### Download compatibility PDF

- Click **Compatibility PDF** (when available)

### Search parts for this model

- Type in the search field
- Submit

### Filter parts by category

- Sidebar or mobile **Filters** (same as browse)

### Sort parts

- New / A–Z / Z–A

### Open a part

- Click a part card

### Change page

- Pagination at the bottom

---

## Parts catalogue (`/parts`)

### Search parts

- Type part name or number
- Submit

### Filter by category

- Sidebar or mobile **Filters**

### Sort the list

- New / A–Z / Z–A

### Open a part

- Click a part card

### Change page

- Pagination at the bottom

---

## Part page (`/parts/...`)

### Go back to model parts list

- Click the back link (model name)

### Change quantity

- Use the quantity dropdown (1–10)

### Add to cart

- Set quantity
- Click **Add to cart**

### Remove from cart (already added)

- Click **Remove from cart**

### Update quantity (already in cart)

- Change quantity in the dropdown — cart updates automatically

### Open compatibility PDF

- Click the model tag under compatible models (when PDF exists)

### Open another part for the same model

- Scroll to **Other parts for …**
- Click a card
