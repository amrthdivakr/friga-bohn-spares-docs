# How to Process and Share Your Quote Invoice
#### [Made by Amruth Divakar with Scribe](https://scribehow.com/o/AmjRagUGQxOh31NKNgqRAQ/viewer/How_to_Process_and_Share_Your_Quote_Invoice__bvfzUJlrSKaQKsHbQFYQgg)
This guide demonstrates how to finalize a purchase order by inputting the necessary reference number and confirming the transaction. You will also learn how to quickly download or share your digital invoice for record-keeping purposes.

1\. Navigate to a [[Quote]] page

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-15/1d4b678d-8b54-4f15-9480-f288a8ca06c2/ascreenshot_fed0afd74e7641f9a0645990993c141d_text_export.jpeg)


2\. Add the Purchase Order number. All other fields will be auto filled with the accounts default address. Update the address if needed.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-15/1d4b678d-8b54-4f15-9480-f288a8ca06c2/ascreenshot_dea6889442e849a8af2ee18e3cbfb416_text_export.jpeg)


3\. Click [[Confirm Order]]  button after filling the form and verifying quote to generate invoice

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-15/40a621b4-55a5-49bc-83c7-e535438c7647/ascreenshot_2fd130c2e53f43f0923ff9be43491085_text_export.jpeg)


4\. Click [[Download Invoice]] to download the invoice PDF document

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-15/f2f0a424-4ef5-4122-8edd-73f0e8bae93d/ascreenshot_37002c90a84c43d2b7632a163dede946_text_export.jpeg)


5\. Click [[Share Invoice]] to share a the invoice PDF document

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-06-15/6bc242b3-4bf0-44d2-9bd2-32a1c9f0eeb5/ascreenshot_674fd295bf9c499d8350d928257f5c53_text_export.jpeg)
