# Site-wide

Available on most pages.

## Go to Home

- Click the logo (header or footer)

## Main menu

- Home
- Models → browse model catalogue
- Parts → parts catalogue
- About → Friga Bohn site about page (new tab)
- Contact → Friga Bohn site contact page (new tab)

## Search shortcut

- Click the search icon in the header → Models browse page

## Open cart

- Click the cart icon (badge shows item count)

## Sign in / open account

- Click the account icon
- If not signed in, you are sent to login
- If signed in, dot on icon; click → account area

## Change language

- Click EN / FR in the header
- Pick English or Français
- Same page reloads in that language

## Footer links

- About, Contact (corporate site)
- Models, Parts
- FAQ / support, order tracking, account (where applicable)
