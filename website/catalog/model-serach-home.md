# How to Search for Models on the Website

This guide provides instructions on how to quickly locate specific product models using the search functionality. Following these steps will help you efficiently filter the catalog to find the exact items you need.

1\. Navigate to **Home** page

![](../images/catalog/model-serach-home/step-01.jpeg)


2\. Click to open the **search selector**

![](../images/catalog/model-serach-home/step-02.jpeg)


3\. Click **Models**

![](../images/catalog/model-serach-home/step-03.jpeg)


4\. Click the **Search box** field.

![](../images/catalog/model-serach-home/step-04.jpeg)


5\. Enter the search term followed by **Enter** or clicking the **search button**


6\. This will take you to the **Models** page with the search results
